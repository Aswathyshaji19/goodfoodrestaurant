ABOUT US

GOOD FOOD Restaurant is located in the heart of New Delhi. We are the leading non vegetarian food chain that is famous for its exotic spices, delicate herbs, and fine dining experience. We have a wide menu list that includes South Indian, North Indian, Chinese, Chaats, and beverages under the categories Hearth Stone (Snacks), Desi Kona (North-Indian), Super Tambi (South-Indian), and Chow-Ho-Jaye (Chinese). We showcase some of its signature innovations in gastronomy. For serving quality Indian food at fair prices, we are counted among best restaurants in India. Good food has spread its aroma in this industry over the past few decades. For serving delicious and lip smacking dishes, Shudh Restaurant has become first choice of foodies among other restaurants in Delhi. All the food on the Good food's menu is cooked to order. To maintain quality and hygiene in food, finest ingredients are delivered to the restaurants daily. Being the best restaurant in New Delhi, Good food Restaurant is committed to provide excellent food, service, and an unforgettable dining experience for you and your family to enjoy for many years to come.
